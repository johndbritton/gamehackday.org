Game

The Assassin/Shadow are masters of getting away alive in World PvP and Warzones once you have the right abilities and use them well. And our damage is fairly good too. We’re not the top-dog in terms of dishing out harm but we really have our moments in PvP with all our abilities. Force Slow, Force Sprint, Resilience, Force of Will on a fairly short cooldown, Deflection helps alot against melee and ranged classes and Force Cloak+Blackout followed by Mind Maze can really piss someone off.

You can put out more damage with an upheaval spec, or obtain more mobility with with digging into the Kinetic tree. Making errors in PvP as a Shadow means death though if you don’t have Force Cloak ready from what I can tell. You are very squishy in shadow tech, so sacrificing a little force sometimes to go combat tech helps a little with being squishy, but you lose the ability to have a hard hitting armor-bypassing Breach then.
